00Manifiesto

Creativity is no longer a discipline. It's a system.

Ideas today don't live inside a single medium. They move across design, motion, storytelling, artificial intelligence and interaction.

A concept can become an image. An image can become motion. Motion can become a tool.

My work exists in that intersection.

Four orbiting nodes of the same practice.

04Tools & Mediums

My work combines design thinking with technical production. The tools change constantly — especially in AI. What doesn't change is the architectural thinking behind them.

AI Video
& ImageGenerative video, image synthesis, and AI-assisted production. Not specific tools — they evolve monthly — but the invisible production architecture: prompt engineering, pipeline design, quality control, and editorial judgment to know when the machine is right and when it needs direction.
